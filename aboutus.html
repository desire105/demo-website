Our Story

Where Vision Meets Craftsmanship

Founded in 2008, OH Architecture emerged from a profound belief that exceptional design should harmonize with human experience. What began as a modest Brisbane studio has evolved into an internationally recognized practice, celebrated for creating spaces that resonate with both aesthetic beauty and functional excellence.

Our philosophy centers on the dialogue between space and inhabitant. We don't merely design structures; we curate environments that enhance daily rituals, foster connections, and stand as testaments to thoughtful innovation.

Our Journey

Studio Evolution

2008

The Foundation

OH Architecture established in Brisbane with a vision to create people-centered, sustainable design solutions for residential and commercial spaces.

2012

Recognition

Received the Queensland Architecture Award for Residential Design, marking our emergence as a leading voice in Australian architecture.

2015

Expansion

Relocated to our current Grange studio and expanded to a team of 12, enabling us to take on larger, more complex projects across Queensland.

2019

Sustainability Focus

Launched our carbon-neutral initiative, committing to sustainable design practices and achieving Green Star certification for all new projects.

2024

Global Reach

Celebrated 15 years with 200+ completed projects, international recognition, and a team of 20 dedicated professionals shaping the future of architecture.
