Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A typical residential project can take 6-12 months from initial consultation to completion, while larger commercial projects may take 12-24 months. We provide a detailed timeline specific to your project during our initial consultation.

What is your design process?

Our design process begins with understanding your vision, needs, and budget. We then move through concept development, detailed design, planning approvals, documentation, and construction administration. We maintain transparent communication throughout every phase to ensure your vision is realized.

Do you work with clients outside of Queensland?

Yes, we work on select projects throughout Australia. While we're based in Queensland, we've completed projects in NSW, Victoria, and Western Australia. Initial consultations can be conducted remotely, with site visits scheduled as needed throughout the project.

What are your fees and how are they structured?

Our fees are typically structured as a percentage of the construction cost or as a fixed fee, depending on the project scope. We provide transparent fee proposals after our initial consultation, outlining all services included. We believe in clear, upfront communication about costs to ensure there are no surprises.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form on this page, calling us at 07 3110 1031, or emailing info@oharchitecture.com.au. We typically respond within 24-48 hours to schedule an initial meeting either in person or via video call.
